Our Story

Crafted for
the Coast

We started Tropical Buzz with one question: why should the best frozen drinks only exist at the bar? We wanted that same quality — real fruit, proper spirits, perfect texture — in a format you could take anywhere.

Every batch is made in small runs in Miami, using whole fruits, no artificial flavors, and no shortcuts. We work with local producers where we can, and we obsess over the details: the right freeze point, the right pour temp, the right balance of sweet, sour, and spirit.

The result is a frozen cocktail that actually tastes like a cocktail — because it is one.

How It Works

No blender. No bartender. No problem.

1
🍹

Choose Your Flavor

Pick from our six signature blends. Mix and match, or go all in on your favorite. Each one is built around a specific spirit and fruit profile.

2
🧊

Keep It Frozen

Store flat in your freezer at 0°F or below. Ready whenever you are — poolside, beachside, or just your own backyard on a Friday night.

3
🌊

Squeeze & Sip

Thaw slightly until soft (about 10 minutes), give it a knead, cut the corner, and pour. Or squeeze straight from the pouch. Either way — enjoy.
